my cousin jake

My name's Jake Fucci, and I'm a 22 year old artist, entrepreneur, rock band manager, men's support group leader, and most importantly, big cousin. In my teenage years I dealt with some heavy mental struggles and came out the other side with a clear sense of who I am and a strong ability to communicate my thoughts and feelings in difficult moments. As the oldest cousin in my family, I've always embraced the chance to set an example and inspire the younger ones to be their best selves in the pursuit of a balanced, joyful life. Now I get to expand that role, working with young men (typically aged 13-20) who just need a mentor in their corner to help them focus on things like:

  • confidence & comfort with yourself
  • navigating anxiety & mental pressure
  • balancing relationships
  • exploring your interests & defining your priorities
  • public speaking & presentation skills
  • how to communicate when something feels wrong
  • outings & experiences for when you just want someone to go with

I'm available in-person in the New York and South Florida areas, and remotely everywhere else. Our conversations stay between you, me, and only those who you give me permission to share info with. I'm not a therapist or doctor, but I'm someone relatable who can offer real support, honest advice, and an open ear. If you ever need more than that, I'll make sure to connect you to the right people in my network of mental health professionals so we can get you exactly what you need.
